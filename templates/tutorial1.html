The basics

Tell Me More is a tool that lets you look up terms in Wikipedia without navigating away from the page you're on. Try it out by selecting "President Obama" in the text below (just click and drag).


In his 2015 State of the Union address, President Obama discussed a variety of topics ranging from policy in Iran to tax loopholes at home.

Once you're done, click anywhere outside the info card to dismiss it.

Obama's not the only term in that snippet above. "2015", "State of the Union", "tax loopholes"...if it's in Wikipedia, Tell Me More will find it.
