The basics

Tell Me More is a tool that lets you look up terms in Wikipedia without navigating away from the page you're on. Try it out by selecting "President Obama" in the text below (just click and drag, like you would if you wanted to copy it).

In his 2015 State of the Union address, President Obama discussed a variety of topics ranging from policy in Iran to tax loopholes at home.

There are plenty of other terms in that snippet -- try selecting any of them. The only limitation is what's found in Wikipedia.
