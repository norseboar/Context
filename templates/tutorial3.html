Disabling auto-show

By default, Tell Me More will show you an info pane whenever you select text (unless it's more than five words). While it's nice to be able to look up terms with just a selection, it's not great for every page. You can disable auto-show for any page by clicking the link in the bottom-right (try it now: select any word, then disable the automatic popup). You can see and edit your list of disabled pages (or disable the auto-show feature entirely) in the options page, which can be found in the extensions management page.


When auto-show has been disabled, you can still look up terms by right-clicking a selection and selecting "Tell me more...".
